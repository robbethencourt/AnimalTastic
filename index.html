aniloop

aniloop strives to raise awareness of endangered animals by letting users see playful gifs created by the Internet community. The site is powered by the Giphy API and was created as a project for the UCF Coding Boot Camp. You can also check out Giphy for other fun gifs.

Donate to the World Wildlife Fund

The animals listed on this site are from the World Wildlife website. They're one of the world's leading conservation organization and work with governments and private companies to conserve nature and reduce the most pressing threats to the diversity of life on Earth. You can learn of their donation options through the Donate button.
